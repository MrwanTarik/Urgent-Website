LIFS/TIZDAN

Libyan International Financial Services Company

LIFS was granted a Electronic Payments License by the Central Bank of Libya and has launched an e-Wallet service called Tizdan. With nearly 1,200 point of sales presences deployed within the first year of service Tizdan is quickly becoming a leader in E-Payments within Libya.

PAYLESS/lA VIE EN ROSE

LIHC is the Libyan franchisee for Payless Shoesource and La Vie En Rose. Our ambitions for our retail segment are incredibly high, stay tuned to learn more about our new brands in the coming months.

LIRCC

Libyan International Real Estate Investment and Construction Company

LIRCC was formed to develop and monetize our extensive real estate portfolio. Projects currently underway include:

  • Forum at Jraba: Libya’s largest privately owned shopping mall in the exclusive Jraba area of Tripoli
  • The Walk: A Lifestyle Project consisting of 559 residential units, a shopping mall and recreational facilities.
  • TP Offices: Office compound consisting of 225 office units and 19 retail spaces.

NORAN

Noran Medical

Noran is a leading pharmaceutical and medical supply distributor in Libya. In November 2021 plans were finalized to open Libya’s largest baby diaper manufacturing facility with a planned opening of March 2023.

LIFZC

Libyan International Company for Trading / LIFZC

LIFZC was formed to handle the logistics needs of trading companies in North Africa, particularly Libya, Tunisia, Algeria, Niger and Chad. With access to over 50 Hectares of storage space, contracts with all major international shipping lines and customs bonded access to the Misurata Airport delivering your products to North Africa has never been easier before!

About LIHC

Libyan International Holding Companies (LIHC), is one of Libya’s largest and most respected privately owned companies. Our subsidiaries are market leaders in the Communications, Technology, Financial, Retail, Real Estate, Medical and Logistics sectors.

Investing locally and developing local talent will allow us to continue to deliver positive change. We are committed to providing training and employment opportunities in Libya, delivering the best possible products to our valued customers and playing our role in diversifying the economy
